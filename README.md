## Bidding Dapp

1. first connect wallet to bid or open a new bid.
2. MyProfile - contains all the baught things
3. +OpenBid - to open a bid (a photo, name, description, opening price, time remaining)
4. for fast bidding do everything off-chain until bidding time is not consumed and at the end whosoever is the highest bidder should confirm the transaction. (till then find a way to get the bid amount into the vault)
5. finally send the bid item to the winner and bid amount to the previous owner.
